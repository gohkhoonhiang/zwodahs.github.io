My lifes

About 3 years ago, I saw a comic about living 11 lifes, by splitting your life into chunks of 7 years. I was truely inspired by it but I think that 7 years is a bit too long.

So I decided to split my life into chunks of 2-4 years, each doing various things, allowing myself to extend or terminate my ‘life’ if necessary. So on average, if you spend about 3 years doing something and If I live until 60, then I would have live about 10 different lifes. Any lifes after 60 is really a bonus.
